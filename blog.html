Font 5 Tool Here | What Is Font 5?
FONT 5 is an online tool for generating cool font texts to be used on any social media to style your text posts to appear fancy. It can also be used in SMS to send very cool styled texts to friends and your beloved ones.
How to Use It?
  • Write any text that you want to style or generate cool fonts for it in the box on top of this page.
  • On typing you will start seeing cool styled fonts are generated on the boxes below the one you are typing.
  • Click a "copy button" beside the font you want and it will be copied on your device clipboard.
  • Finally, go to any platform that you want to post or write a fancy styled text and paste the font you copied
  • *Note: To paste for computer users click Ctrl + V to paste the text. For smartphone's users just hold the text area where you want to post your styled fonts and the option for pasting will appear, click that and your fancy text will be pasted.
    • How Font 5 Works?
  • Texts copied here in Font 5 are like any other texts but they are cool styled for the purpose of impressing your followers or friends with your posts. Just keep in mind that they can be used in any social media network and also on your day-to-day chats with your family and friends
  • Styled texts are generated from popular fontface fonts that are tested to work ony any device and of course not with the old feature phones.
